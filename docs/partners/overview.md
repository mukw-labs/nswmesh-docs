# Partners

These partners provide mesh networking hardware, accessories, or related services that may be useful to NSW Mesh participants.

Partner links are provided as a convenience. Check each partner's current product range, availability, support terms, and shipping details before purchasing.

## Hivemesh

[Hivemesh](https://hivemesh.au) supplies Meshtastic mesh networking gear for Australian projects, including Heltec T114 LoRa starter kits, long-range modules, batteries, and custom 3D printed cases.

Their range is relevant for people building off-grid messaging nodes, hiking or emergency communication kits, IoT sensor networks, and community-owned mesh infrastructure.

Visit: [hivemesh.au](https://hivemesh.au)

## Zindello Industries

[Zindello Industries](https://zindello.com.au) is run by Josh, VK2MES, and provides mesh-related products including the UltraPeater and associated hardware for Australian radio and mesh networking projects.

The site is relevant to NSW Mesh participants looking for locally focused mesh products, repeater information, and related amateur radio resources.

Visit: [zindello.com.au](https://zindello.com.au)
